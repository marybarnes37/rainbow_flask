rainbow spotting tips:

sun

Rainbows only appear when the sun is at 42 degrees or less.

moisture

Rainbows thrive in light showers and mists.

temperature

Rainbows need water, not ice. You won't see any when it's too cold.


about

Infinity Rainbows uses a random forest model to predict when rainbows will appear. The training dataset was assembled based on timestamps from photos of rainbows posted to social media platforms. The website, built with Flask, displays an updated probability each hour based on the most recent weather report. A (future) Twilio integration allows Seattleites to sign up to receive text notifications when there is a high probability of rainbows.

Infinity Rainbows was created by Mary Barnes as a capstone project for the Galvanize Data Science Immersive.
